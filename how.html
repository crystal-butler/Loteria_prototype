  • The tabla WHICH IS THE PICTOGRAM BOARD USED TO PLAY LA LOTERIA, GETS MARKED OFF WITH TOKENS WHENEVER A MATCHING CARD IS CALLED.

  • MARK A MATCHING PICTOGRAM ON YOUR TABLA BY SIMPLY CLICKING ANYWHERE WITHIN THE IMAGE.

  • AS SHOWN ON THE LEFT, ANY FOUR TOKENS FORMING A VERTICAL, HORIZONTAL, OR DIAGONAL ROW, A SQUARE OF ADJACENT TOKENS, OR THAT MARK OF THE OUTSIDE CORNERS RESULTS IN A WIN.

  • WHEN YOU GET A WINNING PATTERN, PRESS THE STAR MARKED ‘LOTERIA!’ TO THE UPPER RIGHT OF YOUR TABLA.

  • IF YOU CALL LOTERIA! FIRST, YOU’RE THE WINNER!
